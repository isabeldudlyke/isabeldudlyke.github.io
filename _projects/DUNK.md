## Two Columns

Markdown doesn't have built-in column support, so you can use small HTML blocks:

<div class="two-col">
  <div>
    **Left Column**
    - Some bullet
    - Another bullet
  </div>
  <div>
    **Right Column**
    - Another list
    - More content
  </div>
</div>
